========================================
DEPLOY TO PRODUCTION - COPY & PASTE
========================================

Open Git Bash or Terminal in your project folder, then copy and paste these commands:

STEP 1: Login to Vercel
------------------------
vercel login


STEP 2: Deploy to Production
-----------------------------
vercel --prod


That's it! Wait 2-3 minutes and all your domains will be updated.
